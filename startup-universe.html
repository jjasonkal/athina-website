Project Management Stakeholder Collaboration Event Planning and Management Marketing Strategy Digital Marketing Content Strategy

As the Country Organizer and Project Manager for the European Startup Universe (present Startup Universe) program in Greece, I embarked on a mission to foster a thriving ecosystem for early-stage startups. This initiative, now rebranded as Startup Universe, aimed to provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to connect, learn, and grow. Leading a dedicated team of 11 individuals, I orchestrated every aspect of the program, from conceptualization to execution. Collaborating closely with organizers from other countries, we worked tirelessly to ensure the success of the 6-week program.

The results spoke volumes about our collective efforts and dedication. European Startup Universe Greece 2022 witnessed an impressive turnout, with 457 Founders, 220 Startups, 45 Experts, and 12 Speakers participating. Moreover, we cultivated strategic partnerships with 4 Strategic Partners, engaged 5 Media Partners, and collaborated with 23 Community Partners, amplifying the program's reach and impact. In line with our commitment to diversity and inclusion, we prioritized female entrepreneurship, with 7 female speakers and 25 female experts actively contributing to the program. Notably, 36% of the participating founders were female, reflecting our efforts to support and empower women in the startup ecosystem.

Our endeavors garnered recognition and support from key stakeholders, with the Greek program receiving patronage from 2 Ministries, 1 Sponsor, and 1 Supporter. Additionally, we facilitated meaningful connections, with 21.1% of Greek Startup Founders meeting Angel Investors through the program. As the Head of Team Greece, I take pride in the success of the European Startup Universe initiative.

It exemplifies our commitment to driving innovation, fostering collaboration, and empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs. I am excited to continue contributing to transformative initiatives that shape the future of entrepreneurship.

Athina Kaltsiki
