ASEM Island Mobility Services Project

Strategic Planning Stakeholder Collaboration Marketing Strategy Social Media Marketing Performance Marketing Marketing Consulting

As the Marketing Project Manager at Dingo Marketing Team, I had the privilege to lead the coordination and execution of the ASEM Island Mobility Services project. This groundbreaking initiative aimed to revolutionize electric vehicle infrastructure in our region. Leveraging my expertise in project management and marketing, I played a pivotal role in driving the success of this innovative venture.

Our goal was ambitious: to establish a comprehensive network of charging stations to support the growing demand for electric vehicles. Through meticulous planning and strategic collaboration, we succeeded in building 20 spots equipped with 36 chargers, making it the largest charging infrastructure of its kind in our area.

One of the project's key achievements was securing the Silver award for Innovation at the prestigious 2019 Tourism Awards. This recognition underscored the project's impact and contribution to sustainable mobility solutions in our community.

My role extended beyond project management; I was responsible for fostering collaboration between Xenakis Autos and the public, ensuring effective communication channels were established and maintained throughout the project lifecycle. Additionally, I led a dedicated team of 5 individuals tasked with creating marketing materials and managing project communications.

It's worth noting a significant aspect of this project: my direct involvement in addressing challenges and mitigating risks, including an incident involving Sophia the Robot. I was present during the incident, which occurred due to human error. While Sophia has legs (although not in use at the event), our proactive approach ensured such incidents were minimized in the future.

Overall, the ASEM Island Mobility Services project exemplifies my commitment to driving impactful initiatives that benefit our community and advance sustainable practices. I am proud to have played a key role in this endeavor, and I look forward to contributing to similar impactful projects in the future.

Athina Kaltsiki
